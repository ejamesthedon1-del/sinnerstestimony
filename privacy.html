Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Sinners Testimony® (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit sinnerstestimony.com and when you sign up for our email and SMS marketing.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for our mailing list or updates, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number (if you opt in to SMS)

We may also collect certain information about your use of our website (e.g., pages visited, device type) through our marketing platform to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you marketing emails about new products, launches, and promotions

Email Marketing

By submitting your email, you consent to receive marketing emails from Sinners Testimony®. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link in any email or by contacting us.

SMS Marketing

If you opt in to receive SMS messages:

  • Message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, HELP for help, or by unsubscribing via the link in our messages.
  • We use an automatic telephone dialing system or similar technology to send messages.
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Third-Party Sharing

We share your information with service providers who help us send emails and SMS messages, including Klaviyo. We will not share your email or SMS opt-in consent with third parties for purposes unrelated to providing you with our marketing services. We may share your data with platform providers, phone companies, and vendors who assist in delivering messages to you.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
